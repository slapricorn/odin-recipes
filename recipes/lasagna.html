Lasagna

Description

The prep time is about 30minutes, followed by 2,5h cooking time. It is made for 12 servings. First you have to make a meat sauce and cook some nudles. Followed by a nice ricotta mixture. The goal is it to layer the lasagna with your ricotta mixture and the meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Tomato products
  • Onion and garlic
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Layers

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
