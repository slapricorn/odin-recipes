Pizza

Description

A recipe for a nice pizza with a fluffy crust and wonderful toppings. It is a recipe for 8 servings. The cook time is about 10mins with 10mins of prep time.

Ingredients

  • 1 unbaked pizza crust
  • 6 tablespoons prepared presto sauce
  • 3 roma tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1 package seasoned goat cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 cup fresh arugula
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven according to pizza package instructions
  2. Put pesto in the center of the Pizza and spread it toward the outer edges.
  3. Cut the goat cheese and crumble it across the pizza.
  4. Arrange tomato slices over the goat cheese.
  5. Sprinkle with garlic.
  6. Brush the crust edges lightly with olive oil.
  7. Place the Pizza in the preheated oven rack.
  8. Wait 5-10min or until the crust edges are golden.
  9. Take the Pizza out of the oven, let it cool down some minutes.
  10. Enjoy!
